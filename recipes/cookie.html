Cookie

Description

These beautiful, golden brown chocolate chip cookies will be all the rave at your next family gathering. Surprise the whole family by bringing these delicious delicacies to the next family court hearing and you're guaranteed to be granted custody!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Leaveners
    • Baking Soda
    • Baking Powser
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Beat sugar, butter, egg and vanilla in one bowl; mix flour, chocolate chips and leaveners in another.
  2. Gradually blend the flour mixture into the butter mixture.
  3. Roll the dough into balls and bake until the edges are golden.
