Lasagna

Description

This delicious dish will have your next dinner guests ravenously devouring this raveshing dish. It would be impossible for anyone with even a semblence of taste to turn down this dish. This lasagna will have your pet lizard lavishly licking down their plate and begging for succulent seconds.

Ingredients

  • Cheese Filling

    • Ricotta Cheese
    • Egg
    • Mozzarella Cheese
    • Parmesean Cheese
    • Italian Seasoning
    • Salt
    • Pepper

  • Meat Sauce

    • Diced Onion
    • Ground Beef (not to be confused with floor beef)
    • Ground Italian Sausage
    • Garlic
    • Chicken Broth
    • Marinara Sauce
    • Tomato Paste

  • Lasagna

    • Lasagna Noodles
    • MORE CHEESE (Optional)

Steps

  1. Combine cheese filling ingredients in a medium bowl and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oul over medium heat and add the diced onion. Let the onions soften for 10 minutes.
  3. Add the ground beef and sausage and increase the heat to medium-high. Cook for 8-10 minutes. Add garlic during the last minute.
  4. Add the chicken broth, marinara sauce, and tomato paste. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer.
  5. Preheat oven to 375°
  6. Begin boiling a large pot of salted pasta water. Once a rapid boil is reached, cook the noodles to al dente. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  7. Lay the noodles flat on wax paper while you begin assembling the lasagna.
  8. Spread one heaping cup of meat sauce on the bottom of a casserole dish.
  9. add lasagna noodles, overlapping them if needed
  10. Spread cheese filling over the noodles followed by more meat sauce.
  11. Top with more lasagna noodles, cheese sauce, then meat sauce.
  12. Finish with more lasagna noodles, the rest of the cheese sauce, and the rest of the meat sauce.
  13. Top with MORE CHEESE (optional).
  14. Bake for 35 minutes.
